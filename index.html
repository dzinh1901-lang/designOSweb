Auto-QA System

Four Layers
of Validation.

Every generation passes through a 4-layer quality control system before reaching the client — with Human-in-the-Loop controls for final creative oversight.

01

Semantic Consistency

CLIP-based scoring validates generated output against the original prompt intent. Flags scenes that deviate from the creative brief.

02

Temporal Stability

Optical flow analysis across all frames. Detects flickering, warping, and frame inconsistency before the clip reaches post-production.

03

Style Consistency

Embedding similarity scoring across frames. Prevents visual drift between scenes and ensures cohesive aesthetic across multi-scene productions.

04

Human-in-the-Loop (HITL)

Scene-level regeneration with adjustable camera, lighting, and style parameters. One click triggers a targeted re-render with Kling 3.0 or a Genspark AI creative variation.
